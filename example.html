France is a country located in the Western part of Europe, bordering countries such as Germany, Spain, and Italy. The capital of France is Paris, renowned for its fashion industry, Art museums, and bakeries. The country is also well known for its exquisite cuisine and wines, especially for many classical dishes served worldwide today. As of June 2018, the population stands at 67.25M, and the land area is 643,801 sq km. The President is Emmanuel Macron, and the National Anthem is La Marseillaise.
